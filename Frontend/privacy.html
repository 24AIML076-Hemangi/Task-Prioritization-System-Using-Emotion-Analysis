Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Task Prioritization System Using Emotion Analysis ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Service.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: email and password hash (never plain text).
  • Task data: titles, importance, urgency, due/reminder timestamps.
  • Optional emotion scan data: emotion label and confidence.
  • Optional reminder delivery data: phone number, reminder preferences.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and maintain accounts.
  • Store and manage tasks.
  • Send reminders by email or SMS if configured.
  • Provide optional emotion-assisted suggestions.

Camera Use

  • The camera is used only when the user manually triggers an emotion scan.
  • Images are processed to detect emotion; no image is stored by the server.

Data Sharing

  • If email or SMS reminders are enabled, data is sent to providers (Gmail API/SMTP/SendGrid/Twilio) only to deliver reminders.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, unless a longer period is required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may choose whether to enable emotion scans and reminders.
  • You can update or delete tasks within the Service.
  • To request account deletion, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above indicates when changes were made.
